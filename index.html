Frozen Yogurt Pops

A cool and refreshing treat perfect for a hot summer day.

Frozen Yogurt Pops

Ingredients:

  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 mashed banana
  • 1/4 cup mixed berries

Instructions:

1. Mash banana and mix with yogurt. 2. Stir in berries. 3. Spoon mixture into ice cube trays. 4. Freeze for a few hours until solid.
